How We Collect Information

How We Use Your Data

We collect and use your information for specific, legitimate business purposes:

Data Storage & Security

How We Protect Your Data: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure Servers: Data is stored on secure, protected servers with regular backups
  • Firewalls: We use firewalls and intrusion detection systems to prevent unauthorized access
